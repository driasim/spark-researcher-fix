# Vibe Incubator Real-World Eval

This chip is only useful if it drives real venture outcomes.

## What Counts As Real-World Validation

- paid pilot
- signed design partner
- active usage from a real operator team
- measurable time saved, revenue created, or workflow reliability improved
- proof that the delivery loop can run with one operator plus agentic support

## What Does Not Count

- liking the thesis
- pretty watchtower pages
- strong heuristic scores alone
- vague founder enthusiasm
- one-off content attention without conversion

## Minimum Promotion Rule

Before treating a venture motion as real incubator doctrine, require:

1. one live customer or operator proof surface
2. one reusable asset created from the launch
3. one post-launch review captured into incubator doctrine
4. one trust/resilience review appropriate to the customer surface
